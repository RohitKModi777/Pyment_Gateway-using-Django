Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the Dashboard. Click on "View Details" for any order to see its current status and timeline.

When will I receive my invoice?

Your invoice is automatically generated once your payment is confirmed. You can download it from the order detail page by clicking the "Download Invoice" button.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets through our secure Razorpay payment gateway.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, please contact our support team using the form above or email us directly. Include your order ID and reason for the refund request. We'll process it within 5-7 business days.

My payment failed. What should I do?

If your payment failed, you can retry the payment from the order detail page by clicking the "Retry Payment" button. If the issue persists, please contact your bank or our support team.

How can I update my account information?

You can update your account information by going to your Dashboard and clicking on "Edit" in the Profile section. You can update your phone number, address, and profile picture.
